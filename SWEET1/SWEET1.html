Description-

Rosogulla is a famous sweet from India. It's like a soft, spongy ball soaked in sugary syrup. It's super tasty and perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings!
Indulge in the iconic delight of Rosogulla, a soft, spongy, and utterly delectable sweet originating from the eastern region of India. Crafted from freshly made cottage cheese dough and simmered in a fragrant sugar syrup, each bite of this melt-in-your-mouth treat is a blissful symphony of sweetness. Whether enjoyed as a comforting dessert or as a celebratory indulgence, Rosogulla embodies the essence of traditional Indian sweets, inviting you to savor every moment of its exquisite flavor and delicate texture.
